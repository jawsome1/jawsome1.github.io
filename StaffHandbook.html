🔒 Staff Regulations

  1. Minimum Player Count for Staff Duty
    Staff may only go on duty when there are at least 15 players online, unless:
    • You witness a bannable offense in real time
    • You have valid proof (e.g., clip, screenshot, or logs)
    If you go on duty under this exception, you must return off duty immediately after resolving the issue.
  2. Uniform & Livery Requirements
    Staff must wear the designated staff uniform and livery while on duty.
    • No custom avatars, VIP skins, or unapproved mods
    • No mixing staff liveries with department liveries
    Failure to comply may result in a warning or removal from duty.
  3. Recording & Interaction Expectations
    We highly recommend that both players and staff record all interactions involving other staff members or players. This helps protect everyone, supports fair moderation, and provides proof when needed.
    Recommended Format: Please record in .mp4 format for compatibility.
  4. Call Response Protocol
    When responding to calls, staff must greet themselves using the standard format provided in the Scripts section. This ensures clarity and professionalism in all interactions.
  5. Kicks & Bans
    When issuing a kick or ban, staff must clearly communicate:
    • What rule was broken
    • How long the punishment lasts
    • Where the player can appeal or provide feedback
    • Where to report the staff member, if necessary (e.g., HR ticket or Supervisor contact)
    Failure to follow these procedures can result in disciplinary action.
  6. Staff Roleplay & Pursuit Policy
    • ❌ Pursuit Involvement: Staff will never join player pursuits unless an offense is actively occurring.
      Valid reasons to intervene include:
      • VDM (Vehicle Deathmatch)
      • RDM (Random Deathmatch)
      • FRP (Fail Roleplay)
      • Other severe behavior
      🚫 Note: Standard GTA-style driving alone does not qualify as a valid reason to join a pursuit.
      Staff may roleplay being pulled over during a pursuit if:
      • They are not interfering with moderation
      • They are not encouraging rule-breaking
    • 🎭 On-Duty Roleplay Guidelines: Staff with this role are allowed to roleplay while on duty, as long as all of the following conditions are met:
      • 🔍 You are not witnessing any offense
      • 📜 Your actions do not break server rules
      • 🚫 You do not use on-duty commands (e.g., teleport, jail, spectate) for roleplay purposes
      • 👁️ You remain aware of your surroundings and ready to respond to incidents
      Violating any of these conditions may result in removal from on-duty roleplay privileges.

Staff Teams & Roles

🦅 Executive Team

Roles: Founder, Chief Operating Officer, Director of Development, Chief Human Resources
  • Oversees the entire structure and vision of the server
  • Approves major decisions (rules, promotions, systems, crises)
  • Manages cross-team coordination and long-term planning
  • Handles final conflict resolution and large-scale changes
  • Reviews and supports staff leadership performance

🧪 Development Team

Roles: Developer, Community Director, Community Developer
  • Builds and maintains bots, scripts, and custom integrations
  • Designs server assets like liveries, uniforms, and templates
  • Maintains systems like Stream Deck tools, website code, and automation
  • Helps troubleshoot tech issues reported by staff
  • Assists in rolling out features or tools across teams

🧠 HR Team

Roles: Lead Human Resources, Human Resources, Junior Human Resources
  • Reviews staff applications and promotions
  • Handles strikes, discipline, and staff concerns
  • Onboards new staff and assists in orientation
  • Maintains internal documentation and logs
  • Collaborates with supervisors and admins on performance reports

📡 Operations Team

Roles: Community Manager, Lead Supervisor, Supervisor
  • Oversees day-to-day staff performance and patrol activity
  • Monitors staff behavior and ensures professionalism during shifts
  • Responds to larger-scale in-game issues when mods/admins escalate
  • Files shift reports, documents patterns in behavior
  • Acts as the “floor lead” for active patrols and roleplay scenes

🎓 Training Team

Roles: Lead Instructor, Instructor, Driving Instructor, Driving Evaluator, Driving Instructor Assistant
  • Hosts training sessions (mod/admin onboarding, patrol procedure, driving tests)
  • Logs completions and results from evaluations
  • Updates and improves training content and SOPs
  • Assists new staff and whitelisted players in learning expectations
  • Supports server professionalism by preparing staff for real situations

🏛️ Admin Team

Roles: Head Admin, Admin, Junior Admin, Trainee Admin
  • Handles escalated situations: bans, teleporting, advanced moderation
  • Responds to tickets that need high-level action
  • Reviews ban appeals and helps track repeat offenders
  • Documents all actions in proper staff logs
  • Supports Moderation and Operations teams when needed

🛡️ Mod Team

Roles: Senior Mod, Mod, Junior Mod
  • Handles basic rule enforcement: warnings, mutes, chat monitoring
  • Responds to player reports and helps de-escalate in-game situations
  • Assists Admins with scenes and logs their moderation actions
  • Monitors behavior during public patrols and events
  • Reports unusual behavior or issues to Admins and Supervisors

🧱 Entry-Level Staff Team

Roles: Trainee Mod, New Staff
  • Shadows active staff to learn procedures
  • Attends required trainings or orientation meetings
  • Observes patrol scenes and ticket responses
  • Cannot take independent action without permission
  • Promoted after sign-off by HR or supervisors
  • Partake in ride-alongs

Outfits/Vehicles

Minimum Requirements While On-Duty:

  • Marked On-Duty in Melonly
    You must be actively listed as on-duty in the Melonly staff system.
  • In Staff Uniform & Vehicle
    Always wear the correct staff uniform and drive an approved staff vehicle.
  • Correct STG (ELS) Pattern Active
    Your ELS lights must reflect your duty status—see chart below.
  • Join the On-Duty Staff VC
    If you're on-duty, you must be in the On-Duty Staff VC, unless you have a VC Exempt role.
  • Actively Patrolling When Not on a Call
    If no mod calls have come in within the past 2 minutes, and you're not on a call, you are expected to patrol.
    This applies unless you hold the Patrol Exempt role or have been directed otherwise by higher command.
  • Follow the Chain of Command
    You must follow the directions of staff ranked above you unless those directions clearly break:
    • PSC:RP rules
    • Server policies or staff handbook
    • Law or common sense (report violations through proper channels)

🚨 ELS / STG Pattern Rules:

STG / ELS Level Usage Description
OFF On break or AFK — not actively staffing.
STG 1 On-duty, patrolling, actively handling or waiting for mod calls, or parked.
STG 2 Monitoring a scene, observing players, issuing a verbal warning or soft stop.
STG 3 Pulling someone over for a kickable or bannable offense — serious action.

Incorrect use of STG levels may result in a warning or strike.

🎖️ EXTRA ROLES

Special permissions and assignments outside the standard CoC. Each of these roles has requirements and must be applied for via a ticket.

🔧 ODC (Off-Duty Commands)

Who Can Apply: Admin+ only
Use Case: For staff needing access to staff tools/commands while off-shift (e.g., dev testing, event setup)
  • Open a ticket
  • Fill out the ODC application form
  • Provide a valid and specific reason
  • Note: Abuse = permanent removal

🔇 VC Exempt

Who Can Apply: Any staff member
Use Case: Unable to join VC regularly due to valid personal reasons (e.g., audio issues, family environment)
  • Open a ticket
  • Fill out the VC Exemption form
  • Must provide honest reasoning — subject to periodic review

🚔 Patrol Exempt

Who Can Apply: Any staff member
Use Case: Cannot regularly patrol due to real-life limitations but still want to support behind-the-scenes
  • Open a ticket
  • Fill out the Patrol Exemption form
  • Provide justification and availability to help in other ways

📄 App Reader

Who Can Apply: Any experienced staff
Use Case: Help review new staff applications
  • Open a ticket
  • Fill out the App Reader application
  • Must be detailed, fair, and trustworthy — quality over speed

🎓 Staff Instructor

Who Can Apply: Trusted Mod+ or current Instructor/Trainer
Use Case: Train new staff or help lead onboarding sessions
  • Open a ticket
  • Fill out the Instructor application
  • Must show communication skills and have clean staff record

🚘 Driving Instructor

Who Can Apply: Staff with solid knowledge of in-game driving and RP law
Use Case: Help run drive tests and assign licenses
  • Open a ticket
  • Fill out the Driving Instructor application
  • Must have a good understanding of realistic traffic law & conduct

🪪 Driver License Applicant

Who Can Apply: Any member (public or staff)
Use Case: Receive an official driver’s license role
  • Open a ticket
  • Request a Driving Test
  • A Driving Instructor will schedule a test with you

🎨 Creative Asset Team

Who Can Apply: Staff or members with digital design experience
Use Case: Contribute to liveries, uniforms, posters, graphics, templates
  • Open a ticket
