Staff Regulations

  • Minimum 15 players required to go on duty (unless offense is witnessed or has proof)
  • Proper uniform/livery required at all times
  • Record staff-player interactions when possible
  • Follow kick/ban protocol: State rule, duration, and appeal process
  • Roleplay allowed while on duty if not interfering with staff responsibilities

Promotions & Infractions

  • Weekly promotions based on activity, behavior, and community feedback
  • No hinting or asking for promotions
  • Infractions = Point System
  • 6 points = Termination risk
  • Minor violations add up over time

Retirement

Staff may retire any time. Admin+ with 5+ quotas can retire officially. Retired staff keep chat access. Open HR ticket to resign or retire.
