Staff Regulations

  • Minimum Player Count: Staff may only go on duty with 15+ players, unless witnessing a bannable offense or with valid proof.
  • Uniform & Livery: Staff must wear designated uniform/livery. No custom avatars, VIP skins, or unapproved mods.
  • Recording: Staff and players are encouraged to record interactions (.mp4 recommended).
  • Call Response: Use standard greeting from Scripts section when responding to calls.
  • Kicks & Bans: Clearly state rule broken, punishment length, and appeal/report info.
  • Pursuit Policy: Staff only join pursuits for active offenses (VDM, RDM, FRP, etc).
  • On-Duty Roleplay: Allowed if not witnessing offenses, not breaking rules, and not using on-duty commands for RP.
